Practice

How we investigate, test, and evaluate emerging interactive systems.

Scope of Work

Hidden Insight Labs works with experimental and emerging technologies that lack established testing standards. Engagements are designed to surface usability issues, technical risk, and deployment constraints before systems reach real users.

What We Work On

  • Augmented and mixed reality applications
  • Spatial and location-based interfaces
  • Prototype and pre-launch systems
  • Interactive installations and experiences

How We Work

Projects are typically short, focused engagements with a clearly defined scope. We emphasize direct testing, field observation, and structured evaluation rather than speculative design.

Findings are documented in written reports intended for technical, product, and leadership audiences.

Deliverables

  • Written evaluation report
  • Identified usability and technical risks
  • Actionable recommendations
  • Optional follow-up discussion
